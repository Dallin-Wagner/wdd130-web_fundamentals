Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase my tennis coaching and racket restringing services while allowing players in the Rexburg area to easily view pricing, schedule lessons, and sign up for private or group sessions.

Audience

My main audience is tennis players in the Rexburg, Idaho area and parents of junior players. A secondary audience is anyone who needs their racket restrung and wants a convenient, local option.

Content

Home Page

About Coach: Coach Dallin “DWag” Wagner has built a strong reputation through his passion for tennis, his understanding of tennis mechanics, and his success in helping players at all levels reach their goals. He provides training sessions for players of all ages that help them develop their skills while building a love for the game, self-discipline, and confidence. His teaching style combines traditional coaching methods with modern tools, including video analysis and basic performance tracking. Students describe his lessons as both fun and challenging while always pushing them to improve.

Training Philosophy: The program is built around the idea that every player is unique. All players are welcome, from complete beginners to experienced competitors, and each student brings their own goals. The Coach DWag approach identifies individual strengths and weaknesses and uses targeted drills to help players reach their potential. Lessons emphasize consistency, good sportsmanship, positive mindset, and learning to compete under pressure.

Contact and Booking: The website will make it easy for players and parents to contact Coach Dallin and book lessons. The contact page will include a simple form, email address, and phone number for direct communication. Visitors will be able to learn how to sign up for group clinics, private sessions, and racket restringing services, as well as what to expect at their first lesson.

Images for the Home page

Testimonials / Scheduling Page

Lessons and Clinics: This page will outline all lesson and clinic options available during the season. It will describe private lessons, small group sessions, and special clinics focused on topics such as serving, net play, and match strategy. Each offering will include information about skill levels, scheduling options, pricing, and how to sign up so visitors can choose the best fit.

Junior Programs: A major focus of this page will be junior tennis programs. Parents will be able to see how clinics help young athletes learn fundamentals through fun, high-energy drills and games. This section will also explain safety policies, supervision, and what equipment players need to bring. The goal is to help kids develop solid technique, confidence, and a long-term love for tennis.

Images for the Testimonials / Scheduling page

Restringing Service Page

Restringing and Equipment Services: This page will explain Coach DWag's local restringing service. It will teach visitors about string types, tension ranges, and how often they should restring based on how frequently they play. The page will clearly list prices, drop-off locations, and typical turnaround times so players know exactly what to expect.

Testimonials and Community: This section will share short testimonials from current and former players and parents. Each testimonial will highlight how coaching or restringing services helped them improve and feel more confident on the court. By reading others’ experiences, new visitors will see that this program values personal growth, hard work, and a supportive tennis community.
